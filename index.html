About Reakgato Solutions

Established in 2018 by Lebogang and Keitumetse Lekwene, Reakgato Solutions is a 100% BEE Level 1 company with 70% women ownership. Our team brings over 20 years of combined experience in the mining industry.

We specialize in comminution, briquetting, compaction, separation and sorting solutions for mining, minerals processing, construction materials preparation, and recycling industries.
