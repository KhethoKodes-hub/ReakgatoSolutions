Our Story

Established in 2018 by Mr. Lebogang Lekwene and Ms. Keitumetse Ellen Lekwene, Reakgato Solutions (Pty) Ltd is a South African company based in Kathu that brings over 20 years of combined experience in the mining industry to every project we undertake.

Our company was founded with a vision to provide exceptional Lining, Crushing and Plant Maintenance solutions and technologies to the mining and minerals processing industry. What started as a business venture with nominal seed capital has grown into a respected provider of specialized engineering solutions.

We pride ourselves on our ability to combine leading-edge technology, state-of-the-art testing capabilities, and extensive in-house experience to provide innovative process solutions to our customers across various industries.
